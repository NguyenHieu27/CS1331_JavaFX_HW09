ADDITIONAL FEATURES:

setOnMouseEntered and setOnMouseExited:

- When the cursor enters one of the labels or spaces in the library, the background color
will change to a lighter tone of gray and the text "Empty" will be changed to "Add (+)".
- When the cursor exits the space, background color and text will return to default.

generateColor():

- A method helper that randomize color rgb for a space background when a song is added.
- Color is ranged not too dark and not too bright.
